Welcome to ChessSavants Lyceum Academy

At ChessSavants Lyceum Academy, we are passionate about chess education and dedicated to helping chess enthusiasts of all levels reach their full potential.


Featured Courses


•Personalized One-on-One Chess Courses for Beginners and Intermediate Players

1.For Beginners:

If you're new to chess, our expert instructors will introduce you to the fundamentals of the game. You'll learn the rules, basic tactics, and essential strategies to build a strong foundation. Through personalized guidance, you'll quickly gain confidence on the chessboard and start enjoying the game to its fullest.


2.For Intermediate Players:

For those already familiar with chess, our one-to-one courses offer the opportunity to advance your skills further. Our experienced coaches will work with you to refine your strategies, enhance your tactical thinking, and deepen your understanding of the game. Whether you're striving to compete in tournaments or simply seeking to become a more formidable opponent, our tailored lessons will help you achieve your goals.


•Group Chess Classes: Learn and Thrive Together

Join our group chess classes and embark on an exciting journey of chess improvement with fellow enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps or an intermediate player seeking to advance your skills, our group classes offer a dynamic and cost-effective way to achieve your chess goals.
