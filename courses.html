Course Title: Beginners' Level Course

Category: Chess Strategy | Level: Beginner | Duration: 6 weeks

Course Description

Chess for Beginners is a comprehensive course designed to introduce newcomers to the world of chess. Whether you're an absolute novice or someone with minimal chess experience, this course will equip you with the foundational knowledge and skills to play chess confidently. Through a combination of lessons, practice exercises, and practical gameplay, you'll learn the rules, strategies, and tactics essential for success in chess.

Course Schedule

  • Week 1: Introduction to Chess Strategy
  • Week 2: Advanced Openings and Middle Game
  • Week 3: Tactics and Combinations
  • Week 4: Positional Play and Endgames
  • Week 5: Game Analysis and Strategy Review
  • Week 6: Final Assessment and Certification
